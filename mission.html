Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be the light of the world and to go and make disciples of all nations and to lead and teach people that Jesus Christ is the only way to salvation upon forgiveness of sins.

Our Vision

The Torch Bearer of Jesus Christ Global Ministries is bound by the very word of God empowered by the Holy Spirit to be the witness in the middle east, and in the Philippines and to the uttermost.
